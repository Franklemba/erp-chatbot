RAG Test Document for ERP Chatbot

1. The ERP system was launched in 2024 to streamline business processes.
2. The HR module allows employees to submit leave applications online.
3. The payroll module processes salaries on the 25th of every month.
4. Vendor onboarding requires submission of a signed contract and company profile.
5. Employee resignation clearance must be approved by the HR manager and the IT department.
6. Procurement SOP mandates at least three vendor quotations for purchases above $1000.
7. The ERP chatbot can answer questions about HR, payroll, procurement, and vendor processes.

This document is for testing retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) in the ERP chatbot system.
